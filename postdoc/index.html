Announcement of a 20-month post-doc position at CentraleSupélec, in the framework of the Inria-EDF Défi on the management of tomorrow's electrical systems. The post-doc will be co-supervized with Francis Bach (Inria Paris), Nadia Oudjane (EDF R&D), and Adrien Séguret (EDF R&D). The post-doc offer deals with the development of new methods for solving large-scale optimization problems, using in particular relaxation techniques with probability measures.
